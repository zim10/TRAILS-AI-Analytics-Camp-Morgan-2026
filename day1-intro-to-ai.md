# Day 1 — Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

**Theme:** What is AI? Where do we see it every day?

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Day 1, students will be able to:
- Define artificial intelligence in plain language
- Identify AI systems in everyday life (social media, navigation, healthcare, etc.)
- Distinguish between narrow AI and general AI
- Describe at least two ways AI is used in business contexts

---

## Lecture Outline (~60 min)

### Part 1: What is AI?
- Definition and history (brief)
- How AI "learns" from data — analogy-based explanation
- Types of AI: rule-based vs. machine learning

### Part 2: AI Around Us
- Examples: recommendation systems, facial recognition, chatbots, fraud detection
- Group activity: "Spot the AI" — students identify AI in scenarios

### Part 3: AI in Business
- Business analytics overview
- How organizations use AI to make decisions
- Introduction to the week's project theme

---

## Lab 1: Exploring AI Tools

**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Tools:** ChatGPT / Claude / Google Gemini (any accessible AI chatbot)

### Instructions
1. Students interact with an AI chatbot to ask it questions about a topic they care about
2. Evaluate the response: Is it accurate? How do you know?
3. Try to "trick" the AI — what happens when you give it unclear or misleading input?
4. Record observations on the Lab 1 worksheet

### Reflection Questions
- What surprised you about how the AI responded?
- What did the AI get wrong or seem confused about?
- Would you trust this AI to make an important decision? Why or why not?

---

## Team Formation

- Students are assigned to teams of 4–5
- Each team selects a **business problem** from a provided list (e.g., healthcare, transportation, retail)
- Teams will work on this problem across the week, culminating in a Friday showcase presentation

---

## Pre-Assessment Survey

Administered at the start of Day 1 (before the lecture). Measures:
- Prior exposure to AI tools
- Initial attitudes toward AI trustworthiness
- Self-reported digital literacy
