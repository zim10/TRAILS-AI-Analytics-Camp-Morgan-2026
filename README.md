## 📌 About This Program

**TRAILS AI & Analytics High School Camp** is a five-day summer workshop hosted by the [Earl G. Graves School of Business & Management](https://business.morgan.edu/) at Morgan State University. The program introduces Greater Baltimore high school students to Artificial Intelligence, Business Analytics, and responsible technology use — with a strong focus on **trustworthy AI**.

> This initiative supports **Transformation Morgan 2030** (Goal 5: Anchor Institution in Baltimore) and the Graves School strategic goals of thought leadership and economic equity.

## 🎯 Program Goals

- **Increase digital literacy** among underrepresented Baltimore youth
- **Expose students** to AI and data analytics in real business contexts
- **Build trustworthy AI awareness** — reliability, fairness, privacy, explainability, and accountability
- **Inspire pathways** to higher education and careers in technology and business
- **Connect students** with faculty mentors, current college students, and industry professionals

## 📚 Trustworthy AI Framework

This workshop embeds the following **AI trustworthiness pillars** into every module, lab, and project:

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| ✅ **Reliability/Accuracy** | How do we evaluate whether an AI is correct? |
| ⚖️ **Fairness** | Who benefits and who might be harmed by AI decisions? |
| 🔐 **Privacy** | What data does AI use, and how is it protected? |
| 🔍 **Transparency** | Can we understand *why* an AI made a decision? |
| 📣 **Accountability** | Who is responsible when AI gets it wrong? |

---

## 🏆 Closing Showcase

On Day 5, student teams present their projects to a panel of **Graves faculty, Morgan students, and industry partners**. Teams are judged using a rubric built around the trustworthy AI pillars above.
